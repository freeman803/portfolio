Wiring & Harness

Electrical Design: HV Battery Pack Wiring — Concordia Formula Racing

  • Designed the high-voltage (HV) battery pack wiring architecture, ensuring all required power and signal connections
  • Fully routed all pack wiring, optimizing layout for minimal length, reduced resistance, and efficient packaging within tight spatial constraints
  • Implemented optimized wire routing strategies to improve serviceability, reduce electromagnetic interference (EMI), and maintain clear separation between HV and low-voltage systems
  • Ensured full integration and functionality of the safety circuit, including proper interlocks, shutdown paths, and fault detection mechanisms
  • Selected appropriate wire gauges, insulation, and connectors based on current demands, voltage levels, and thermal considerations
  • Verified harness integrity and continuity through systematic testing, ensuring reliability under operational conditions
  • Contributed to overall system safety and reliability by aligning wiring design with best practices and competition regulations
  • Ensured compliance with the FSAE ruleset
