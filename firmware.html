Firmware

Electrical & Firmware Integration — Concordia Formula Racing

Source code: github.com/concordia-fsae/firmware

  • Edited and expanded CAN messaging within firmware to support new subsystems, improving communication reliability and system-level integration
  • Implemented additional CAN messages for enhanced data visibility, enabling improved logging, diagnostics, and real-time system monitoring
  • Developed and mapped driver-facin messages to the display, significantly improving the driver interface with clearer system feedback and fault indication
  • Brought up and validated shock displacement sensors, including calibration procedures to ensure accurate suspension travel data
  • Commissioned and calibrated the VC Front system, verifying proper operation and integration within the overall vehicle control architecture
  • Brought up the Tractive System Safety Indicator (TSSI), ensuring correct behavior and compliance with safety rules
  • Updated current scaling (mV/A) for the BOSS system based on datasheet specifications, improving measurement accuracy
  • Integrated motor controller CAN messages into the YAMCAN network, enabling full decoding and logging of drivetrain data for debugging, validation, and performance tuning
